I am currently a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at Zhejiang University of Technology, expected to graduate in June 2026.

Following my undergraduate studies, I have been admitted to pursue a Ph.D. degree at the Shanghai Institute for AI for Education, East China Normal University, under the supervision of Associate Professor Fei Tan. My admission was granted through a recommendation-based exemption.

My primary research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and the application of Artificial Intelligence in Education (AIED). I am deeply passionate about these fields and look forward to contributing to the advancement of LLMs and the enhancement of educational quality through my continuous research efforts.

Additionally, I have actively participated in top-tier programming competitions, including the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) and the China Collegiate Programming Contest (CCPC), securing two Bronze Medals at an ICPC Asia Regional Contest.

Previously, I explored fields such as Time Series Anomaly Detection and Industrial Control System (ICS) Security; however, these are no longer my current research focus.

For any inquiries or potential collaborations, please feel free to contact me via email at: zhengnan.guo [at] zjut.edu.cn.
