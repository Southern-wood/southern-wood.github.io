About

Hi there! I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at Zhejiang University of Technology, expecting to graduate in June 2026.

I have been admitted to the Ph.D. program at the Shanghai Institute of Artificial Intelligence for Education, East China Normal University. I will be conducting my research under the supervision of Prof. Fei Tan, with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Artificial Intelligence in Education (AIED). My recent interests include Diffusion Language Models, Evaluation of LLMs, and Hallucination Mitigation for LLMs.

I am always open to academic discussions, potential collaborations, or simply making new friends—drop me a message anytime! Email: zhengnan.guo [at] zjut.edu.cn
